aworx::lib::util Namespace Reference

Programmer's Manual

This ALib namespace holds rather simple utility classes which on the one hand do not 'deserve' to be placed in a separated own namespace and on the other hand are not close enough to the C++ language to be placed in namespace aworx::lib::lang.
